# Sum Two Integers

## When to Use
- The user asks to add two numbers, sum two integers, or compute a simple sum.

## Instructions
1. Identify the two integers in the request.
2. Compute the sum.
3. Respond exactly in this format: `SUM=<number>` (no extra text).
